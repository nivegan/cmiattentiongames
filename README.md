# cmiattentiongames

Resist the attention economy with daily games.
Kalari is an editorial, minimalist cognitive puzzle platform, designed to challenge and visual focus, working memory, and pattern recognition. With a daily selection of attention-based challenges crafted to stretch cognitive limits, we aim to train users at having better bias detection and, as the name suggests, attention, with the help of fun games.

## Sensory & Attention

### Steady Gaze | Training Pure Awareness

Identify the different coloured dot on the screen, but keep your eyes peeled, they come and go, and panic taps will incur a penalty. Two misses and its game over. New colour schemes and dots every day.

### Clear the Air | Dissolving Distractions

Pop black "distraction" bubbles as they grow but avoid red "calm" bubbles. If a black bubble reaches max size, it "smudges" that area of the screen, obscuring the field. The quicker you respond to popping black bubbles, the faster it gets.

## Logic & Calibration

### Extract the Facts | Separating Bias

Read two short paragraphs on the same topic with opposing narratives. Complete 3 MCQs to identify verifiable, neutral facts vs. spin. New set of topics, paragraphs and questions every day, ranging from tech launches and local policy to historical events.

### Gut Check | Calibrating Confidence

Three rounds of numerical estimation. Receive an Anchor Statement, answer a Y/N question, then guess the actual number, and submit your confidence. With new topics and questions everyday.

## Reflex & Cognitive Flexibility

### Read Between the Designs | Detecting Manipulation

Identify the dark design pattern among text, Ui, ad, and graphs and the manipulation tactic. Learn deceptive and manipulative patterns with a new set every day.

### Mental Reflex | Breaking Automaticity

Game rule changes every 15 seconds, can you keep up?
